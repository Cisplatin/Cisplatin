About Me

I'm a 21-year-old with terminal osteosarcoma. This website was initially created as a portfolio for my career, but I am now transforming it into the last remenant of myself. The main project that I want to leave behind is my final testament. I consider it my life in a book - a splatter of my favorite memories, thoughts, lists of drugs I've done, and whatever else I decide was important in my life.
