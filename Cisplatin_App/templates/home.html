About Me

I'm a student at the University of Waterloo, studying computer science. My main focus is security, but I also study topics like graph theory, cryptography, bioinformatics, and full-stack development.

I'm an avid chess player, with an estimated FIDE rating of 1400. Over the past two years I've played an average of 5 games a day. I also take part in bug bounty programs, in which I look for security vulnerabilities in popular websites. Besides computer science, I enjoy weightlifting, and am an aspiring competitive bodybuilder.
